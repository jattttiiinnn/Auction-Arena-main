Support Us

Help us enhance the Online Auction experience!

1.) "What is Auciton Arena?"

Auciton Arena is an online auction platform where users can bid on a variety of items in real-time. Our platform provides a secure, transparent, and interactive way to participate in auctions from anywhere.

2.)"How do i create an account?"

To create an account, click the “Sign Up” button on the homepage. Enter your details, verify your email, and set up your profile to start bidding.

3.)"How do auctions work on the platform?"

Once registered, you can join active auctions by selecting an item of interest. Place your bid, and if your bid remains the highest by the auction end time, you win the item.

4.)"What payment methods are accepted?"

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. Full payment details will be provided during the checkout process.

5.)"Is there a fee for participating in auctions?"

Registering on our platform is free. However, some auctions may have listing fees or a small percentage commission on the final sale price.

6.)"What happens if I win an auction?"

If you win an auction, you’ll receive a confirmation email with payment instructions. Once the payment is processed, the seller will ship the item to you according to the agreed-upon delivery terms.

7.)"How can I track my bids?"

You can track all of your active bids through the “My Bids” section under your user dashboard. We also send real-time notifications when you’ve been outbid or won an auction.

8.)"What happens if I lose an auction?"

If you are outbid, you’ll receive a notification. You can try again on another item or bid on a similar one.

9.)"How do I list an item for auction?"

To list an item, go to your dashboard and select the “List an Item” option. Follow the steps to add item details, set a starting price, and choose an auction duration.

10.)"Can I cancel a bid?"

Bids are generally binding, but under exceptional circumstances, you can request a bid cancellation by contacting our support team. Please review the auction terms before.

11.)"What is the refund policy?"

Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. Please refer to our refund and dispute resolution policy for more details, or contact customer support if you encounter issues with a transaction.

12.)"How do I contact customer support?"

For any inquiries or assistance, scroll down to contact info and fill the form. You can also reach out via live chat during business hours.
